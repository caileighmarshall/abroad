Welcome to my blog

Edinburgh, Scotland - September 4, 2017

Greetings from Scotland!

As you can see, this is my first blog post of the semester. I figured I would make a blog to document the places I go and things I do while abroad, but I wanted to make my blog a little more interesting than just images and text. Tapping into my computer science and web design background, I decided not only to code my blog by hand but also to add an interactive map so you could explore Europe with me!

The map is a super cool feature that you can use to follow me throughout the semester. If a city or country is colored red, then I have visited that location and have written at least 1 blog post about it. Clicking on a country will allow you to view all the blog posts from that country and clicking on a city will allow you to view all the blog posts from that city. If you just want to see where I've been chronologically, I also included a link to view my blog posts by date.

I will try to update the blog on a weekly basis, though the day it is posted might differ week-to-week. Because of this variability, I plan on posting links to new blog posts on both Facebook and Instagram so you don't miss out on my travels.


Cheers!

- Caileigh Marshall
